ABOUT THE COLLECTION

The RISD museum collection has more than 100,000 works of art and design dating from ancient times to today. Of these objects, 2,300 of them are on view in the museum now. 82,196 of them are available online. There are 709 recent acquisitions. All of our collection records are living documents. They are frequently revised and enhanced.
